Trademark Registration in India ™️

Complete Guide for Beginners

What is a Trademark?

A trademark is a unique symbol, name, logo, or phrase used to identify your brand and distinguish it from others.

What Can Be Registered?

  • Brand Name
  • Logo
  • Slogan / Tagline
  • Sound Mark
  • Shape of Goods

Eligibility Criteria

  • Must be unique
  • Should not be identical to existing trademarks
  • Must not be offensive or misleading

Trademark Registration Process

1. Trademark Search
Check if your brand name/logo is available.
2. Choose Class
Select the correct trademark class (45 classes available).
3. File Application
Submit Form TM-A online.
4. Examination
Government reviews your application.
5. Publication
Published in Trademark Journal.
6. Registration
Trademark is granted ® 🎉

Cost of Registration

  • Individual / Startup: ₹4,500
  • Company: ₹9,000
  • Professional Fees: ₹5,000 – ₹20,000

Timeline

  • TM symbol (™): Immediately after filing
  • Registration (®): 6–18 months

Pro Tips

  • Choose a unique and creative name
  • Always check availability before filing
  • Use ™ after applying
  • Renew trademark every 10 years
