Patent Filing in India 🇮🇳

Complete Guide for Beginners

What is a Patent?

A patent gives you exclusive rights over your invention for 20 years, preventing others from using it without permission.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Novelty: Must be new worldwide
  • Inventive Step: Not obvious
  • Industrial Use: Must be usable

What Cannot Be Patented

  • Abstract ideas
  • Business methods
  • Algorithms
  • Natural discoveries

Patent Filing Process

1. Patent Search
Check if your idea already exists.
2. Draft Application
Includes description, claims, and drawings.
3. File Application
Provisional or Complete.
4. Publication
Happens after 18 months.
5. Examination
Request via Form 18.
6. Grant
Patent is approved 🎉

Cost of Filing

  • Government Fees: ₹1,600 – ₹8,000
  • Professional Fees: ₹10,000 – ₹50,000+
  • Total: ₹15K – ₹80K

Timeline

  • Fast Track: 1–2 years
  • Normal: 3–5 years

Pro Tips

  • File provisional early
  • Do not disclose idea publicly
  • Focus on strong claims
  • Hire a patent agent if possible
